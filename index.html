ॐ नमः पार्वती पतये हर हर महादेव

My dear special one ❤️,

How can I tell you that your smile, your eyes, your presence, just everything about you, I love. In my eyes, you are totally perfect. You are my world, my life, you mean a lot to me. I will never let you go because I know my life won’t be complete without you in it.

I love you with all my heart ❤️.
